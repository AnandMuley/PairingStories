Rule2 - Three of a kind:

High card is the highest rank card a player has got.

Scenario 1 : If a player has got three cards of the same rank then he wins.

Input:
P1 (John) : JACK-CLUB, JACK-SPADE, JACK-DIAMOND


P2 (Bob) : ACE-SPADE, KING-HEART, THREE-DIAMOND

Output:
John has won by three of a kind: JACK

Scenario 2 : If more than one player has got three of a kind, the player having higher rank three of a kind wins.

Input:
P1 (John) : JACK-CLUB, JACK-HEART, JACK-DIAMOND


P2 (Bob) : ACE-CLUB, ACE-HEART, ACE-SPADE

Output:
Bob has won by three of a kind: ACE
