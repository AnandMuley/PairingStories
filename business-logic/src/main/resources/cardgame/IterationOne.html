Rule 1 – High Card:

High card is the highest rank card a player has got. If a player has got a high card with rank greater than that of the other players, then he wins.

Input:
P1 (John) : TEN-CLUB, JACK-HEART, JACK-DIAMOND
P2 (Bob) : NINE-CLUB, THREE-HEART, EIGHT-DIAMOND

Output:
John has won by high card: JACK – HEART
