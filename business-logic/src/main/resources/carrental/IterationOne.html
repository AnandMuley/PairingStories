A vehicle can be rented for a trip.Vehicle can be a car, SUV or bus. The standard rate for a petrol vehicle for a standard trip is 9 rs/km. Additional 2 rs/km charge is applicable for AC vehicles. Diesel vehicles cost a rupee less than standard rate.

SUV and bus always run on diesel.SUV is always AC.

2% discount is applicable for bus on standard rate.

Calculate the total expense for the trip.

The route of the trip always passes via Pune. All the distances to all destinations are specified in KM from Pune.

Sample Input:
Distance:

  • Pune: 0KM
  • Mumbai: 200KM
  • Bangalore: 1000KM
  • Delhi: 2050KM
  • Chennai: 1234.5KM

Trip:Swift Diesel NON_AC, Pune-Mumbai
Output:Total Expense: 1600

Trip:"Mahindra XUV 500", Mumbai-Bangalore
Output:Total Expense: 12000

Trip:"Tata Skyline", AC, Mumbai-Bangalore
Output:Total Expense: 11760
